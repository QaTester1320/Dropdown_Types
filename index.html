Locator Quick Reference

Use these attributes in your Java/Selenium test code:

  • **By.id():** `standardDropdown`, `multiSelectDropdown`, `searchComboInput`, `L2Frontend`
  • **By.name():** `standard_selection_name`, `city_combo_name`, `inline_dropdown_name`
  • **By.className():** `inline-select-class`, `dropdown-list-component`
  • **By.cssSelector() / By.xpath():**
    e.g., `button[data-selenium-test='btn-target']` or `div[data-test='floating-menu']`
